Services We Offer:

Ground Transportation

All ground transportation is round trip affairs. Ground transportation on the Big Island is done by our staff and an outside company. The team will provide you with a comfortable ride in a well-air-conditioned motor coach that will pick you up at the airport and take you to your hotel and activities while you are still on the island. Note: Airfare is done by a seperate group travel agency and the team will coordinate with them.

Lodging

Lodging in Hilo is done right in the heart of Hilo Bay! The Hilo Hawaiian Hotel is a beautiful estate that sits right on Hilo Bay near the Lil'uokalani Gardens and Coconut Island. Lodging on O'ahu is done right in the middle of Waikiki at the Courtyard by Marriott Waikiki Beach. Brent and his staff at the Courtyard provide an excellent stay and the location is close to everything! Only a five-minute walk to Waikiki Beach.

Activities

Along with your games and practices, your team will get the opportunity to see the historic sites of O'ahu. TitleTown offers three different travel packages: Basic, Standard, and Premium. If you want to stay in Hilo for the entire stay we can create activity packages for you to fit your team's activity needs on the Big Island. Other accommodations can be arranged before the contract is signed!
